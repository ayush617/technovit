Vellore Institute of Techonology, Chennai

Acclaimed as one of the best private universities in India, Vellore Institute of Technology, VIT was founded in the year 1984 by the visionary Dr. G. Viswanathan. In a short span, VIT has witnessed a meteoric rise and set an ambitious growth target. This was manifested in its towering presence in technical and management education, state-of-the-art infrastructure that meets global standards and the scholastic faculty who are a cynosure in the academic realm. The piloting principle is sustained innovation in education, administration and social responsibility.
